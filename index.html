Program Overview

Our BTech in Computer Science Engineering program is designed to provide a strong foundation in computer science principles, programming languages, algorithms, data structures, and system design. The curriculum integrates theoretical knowledge with practical applications, preparing students for the dynamic and evolving technology landscape.

The program spans 8 semesters over 4 years, offering a blend of core courses, electives, and industry-relevant projects. Students will gain expertise in various domains including software development, artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, web and mobile technologies, cybersecurity, and cloud computing.

With state-of-the-art laboratories, industry partnerships, and experienced faculty members, we ensure that our students receive a world-class education that prepares them for successful careers in the global tech industry.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements for the BTech program?

Admission requires successful completion of 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, securing a minimum aggregate of 70% (may vary). Candidates must qualify through the Sri Sri University Entrance Examination (SSUCET) or possess valid scores in national exams like JEE Main/Advanced. Please refer to the official admissions brochure for detailed criteria.

Are there scholarship opportunities available?

Yes, Sri Sri University offers a range of scholarships. These include merit-based scholarships for top performers in entrance exams/academics (up to 100% tuition waiver), need-based financial aid programs, and special scholarships for promoting diversity (e.g., women in tech, specific regions). Visit our scholarship portal for details and application procedures.

What internship opportunities are available?

A mandatory industry internship (typically 6 months) is integrated into the curriculum, usually during the 8th semester. Also during Summer Vacation we provide 2-Month Internship Opportunities too. Our Career Services office actively collaborates with over 200+ companies (including MNCs and startups) to facilitate these internships. Students receive support in finding opportunities aligned with their interests and career goals.

Can I pursue higher studies (Masters/PhD) after this program?

Absolutely. Our BTech CSE program provides a robust theoretical and practical foundation, making graduates highly competitive for postgraduate studies globally. Many alumni have successfully pursued MS and PhD degrees at top-tier universities in the US, Europe, and Asia. Faculty actively mentor students interested in research and higher education pathways.

What is the student-faculty ratio?

We maintain a healthy student-faculty ratio, typically around 15:1 for core CSE courses. This ensures personalized attention, effective mentoring, and ample opportunities for students to interact with professors during lectures, labs, and project work.
